# E-Marchi

E-Marchi is a simple and elegant e-commerce website template designed to showcase and sell your products online. With its clean and responsive design, E-Marchi provides a seamless shopping experience for your customers.

![E-Marchi Preview](./preview.png)

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Ensures optimal viewing experience across various devices.
- **Product Showcase**: Display your products with high-quality images and detailed descriptions.
- **Shopping Cart**: Easily add products to the cart and proceed to checkout.
- **Dynamic Counter**: Keeps track of the number of products added to the cart in real-time.
- **Hover Animations**: Interactive hover effects for product descriptions.
- **Easy Customization**: Simple HTML, CSS, and JavaScript structure for easy customization.
